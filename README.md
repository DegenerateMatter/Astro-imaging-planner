# 🔭 Astro Imaging Planner Pro V1.0

**The ultimate planning engine for serious deep-sky astrophotography.** Stop guessing how much integration time you'll actually get tonight. This app simulates your entire imaging run, including gear overheads, atmospheric distortion, and lunar interference, to give you the "True" integration time you can expect to capture.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### ⏱️ Night Sequencer
Plan tonight's multi-target run with high-precision forecasting:
* **True Integration Math:** Factors in your sub-exposure length plus dither settle times.
* **Gear Overheads:** Automatically accounts for **Meridian Flip** downtime and slew/solve settle times.
* **Forecasted Autofocus:** Simulates routines based on your specific **Time** or **Temperature Drop** triggers.
* **Interactive Checklist:** Built-in "Launch Checklist" to ensure you never forget to turn on dew heaters again.

### 📅 Campaign Strategy Planner
Planning a 30-hour project like **Rho Ophiuchi**? 
* **Weekend Warrior Mode:** Select only the days you are available to shoot (e.g., Friday/Saturday).
* **Lunar Intelligence:** Automatically skips nights where the moon is too close or too bright for your chosen filter type.
* **Bortle Reality Check:** Converts your backyard hours into "Dark Site Equivalent" hours.

---

## 🛠️ Quick Start
1. **Set Location:** Enter your Lat/Lon and Timezone in the sidebar.
2. **Define Gear:** Input your meridian flip and autofocus downtime.
3. **Pick a Target:** Use catalog IDs (M42, IC 4604) or common names.
4. **Follow the Verdict:** Look for the **Green Diamond 💎**—that's your signal for a perfect imaging night.
